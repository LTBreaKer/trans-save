    In Tag Game, two players navigate a 2D space with the goal of tagging each other. Player 1 moves using the keys W, A and D, while Player 2 uses the arrow keys (Up, Right, Left). The player who is currently the "tagger" is marked by a white arrow above their character and moves slightly faster. When the tagger collides with the player being chased, their status changes, and a "go" arrow appears above them, indicating a 3-second cooldown period. This cooldown gives the player being chased a brief head start, creating a more balanced and exciting chase.
